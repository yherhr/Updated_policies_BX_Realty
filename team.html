Ellie Begg

Head of Sales

Ellie Begg is the Director and Head of Sales at BX Realty, bringing a modern, strategic and client focused approach to real estate across the Bathurst Region.

With a background in media and marketing, Ellie combines innovative campaign strategies with strong negotiation skills to help properties stand out in an increasingly competitive market. Prior to real estate, she worked within leading media agencies managing campaigns for national and international brands, giving her a strong understanding of buyer behaviour, digital marketing and brand positioning.

Since transitioning into real estate, Ellie has built a reputation for her honest communication, strong work ethic and hands on approach throughout every stage of the sales process. Specialising in residential sales throughout Bathurst and surrounding areas, she is passionate about delivering tailored strategies that maximise exposure while keeping the experience straightforward and stress free for her clients.

As Director of BX Realty, Ellie is committed to creating a forward thinking agency experience built on strong service, genuine relationships and innovative marketing.
